The University Institute of Technology

In France, 113 institutes of technology are spread over the country. This ensures a strong foothold in the local and regional economic environment. to ensure cohesion at the national level, meetings between the department directors are organized.

For nearly 40 years, the IUT had a quality of education recognized and plebiscited by the companies, thanks to the implementation of programs adapted to the professional reality and the creation of diplomas in response to the emergence of brand new professions.

The courses cover most sectors (secondary and tertiary) and propose the collaboration of de professionals, and make it the strength and success of the IUTs. These courses allow students to find a job within 6 months after graduation.

This diversification of diplomas and courses leads to the implementation of pedagogical modes which are suitable for a greater number of students and professionals who want to go back to studies or do a Validation of Acquired Experience.

In addition, being in partnership with foreign universities, the IUTs are proposing options ton continues studies abroad, as well as internships.

The IT Department

At the IUT, the teaching of computer science is mainly focused on the professional world but keeps fundamental components; the student then acquires know-how and knowledge. In a professional situation, basic concepts and working methods can be put to good use.

The DUT, university and professional diploma (level L2 of the LMD scheme, 120 European credits) :

Holders of the "DUT" in Computer Science are senior technicians capable of participating in design, realization and implementation of computer systems. A continuation of their studies at the end of this diploma may be possible, if they wish.

The SIL Professional License (Computer Systems and Software) is a level L3 diploma in the scheme LMD (Bachelor's degree, 180 European credits). Professional licenses are offered in the computer science departments of the IUTs.

SIL licensees are specialists capable of participating in a software development project and, eventually, to take full responsibility for it.

Validation of knwoledge :

It is also possible to have one's experience recognized in order to obtain a diploma, title or certificate of professional qualification. Concerning the validation of the acquired experience, you can visit the government site.
