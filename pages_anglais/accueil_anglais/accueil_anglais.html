OVERVIEW :

The "DUT" in computer science of France train the students to computer science and to the concept of team work, in order to prepare them to a good integration in professionnal life.

There is small-scale training, with available teachers and small working groups, to allow better contact between teaching teams and students. The students have access to personalized and pedagogical teaching.

THE 3 AXIS :

    Teaching in computer science is based on 3 axes, requiring specific qualities and skills:

  • Analysis and design: communication with the client in order to define the objectives and expectations of a project, and to design its structure;
  • Algorithms and Programming : design of the IT project, from processing to coding ;
  • System and network architecture : Learn how a computer works, from its components to its operating system and computer networks.

In addition to these learning axes, general subjects such as mathematics, economics, management, English or communication are taught, making the computer science "DUT" a solid and diversified training. Finally, the learning and evaluation of skills is not only based on theory but also on practice, with simulations, tutored projects or an internship in a company, in order to solidly validate the knowledge and skills acquired..
