The development team

This website is the result of a tutored project carried out by a team of three students, Anne-Sophie Hovart, Jean Dombrowski and Nicolas Goguillon, students at the IUT of Lens, at the IT department.

The aim of the project was to produce a new version of the national portal of the IUTs. Our goal was to create a site that was simpler, cleaner, clearer and easier to use.
